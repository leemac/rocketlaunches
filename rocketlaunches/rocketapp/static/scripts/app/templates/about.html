About

RocketLaunches.net aims to provide visitors with information regarding Rocket Launches around the world. The end goal is to provide interesting information about launches, manufacturers and the rockets themselves.

This site was created by Lee McKinnon (that's me). I created this site after many years of being fascinated by space travel and the technology behind it. I wanted to start this project as I saw an opportunity to create a one-stop-shop for all things rockets, the launches and the industry as a whole.

The initial version (1.0) was developed entirely during Christmas week of 2014 and it went public in January 2015. It is currently pretty basic, but I hope to add more functionality very soon.

I will carefully update the data over time and will add features as requested. The source of the data varies from all over the web.
